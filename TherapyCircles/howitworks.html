Receive free grief counseling from
the comfort of your home.

1. Create a profile

Creating an account takes only a few steps and is done in a minute, and then get an assessment to identify your counseling needs. Your information is then stored securely for all future online visits.

2. Match with a provider

Choose the counselor that works best for you. We’ll help you match you with the counselors based on availability, your preference, and their experience before starting your choice.

3. Begin Session

Once matched, schedule your live video or audio session. You can request your choice anytime, anywhere.
